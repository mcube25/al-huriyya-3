    Al-Huriyya Project

    Support a child scheme

    • To work with qualified healthcare professionals to provide enlightenment on health issues related to children.
    • To work with qualified healthcare professionals to provide affordable access to qualitative peadiatric health service which will in shaa Allaah, reduce infant mortality rate.
    • Sponsor An Orphan Scheme: A child living in an approved orphanage will be chosen for this scheme. The child will have all his medical, feeding, clothing, and educational fees paid until he/she leaves the university and is working in a chosen field of endeavour.
    • Islam For Schools Scheme: Chosen schools will have textbooks, CD-roms, Computers, and teachers (where necessary) for the teaching of qualitative Islamic education.
    • Sponsor A School Scheme: Selected Muslim schools will get donations in form of books, stationeries and computers.
